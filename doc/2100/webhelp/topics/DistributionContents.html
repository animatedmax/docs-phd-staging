Distribution Contents

Pivotal HD is a full Apache Hadoop distribution with Pivotal add-ons and a native integration with Pivotal HAWQ.

The RPM distribution of PHD contains the following:

  • Hadoop 2.2.0
  • Pig 0.12.0
  • Zookeeper 3.4.5
  • HBase 0.96.0
  • Hive 0.12.0
  • Hcatalog 0.12.0
  • Mahout 0.7
  • Flume 1.4.0
  • Sqoop 1.4.2
  • Oozie 4.0.0
  • Hamster 1.1
  • GraphLab 2.2
